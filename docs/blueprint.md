# **App Name**: ConvoTag Lite

## Core Features:

- Profile Creation and Management: Enable users to create and manage their profiles with essential information such as photo, role, company, and contact details.
- QR Code Generation: Generate QR codes for digital business cards to facilitate easy sharing.
- Card Scanning: Scan QR codes of other users to quickly save their contact information.
- Card Sharing via Link: Allow users to share their digital business cards via a shareable link.
- Background Generation: Generate novel backgrounds for digital business cards, based on an analysis of a company website, so that all cards match their corporate branding. LLM acts as a tool that extracts logos or style information from URLs, and uses it to generate prompts for a stable diffusion service.
- Contact List: Keep an updated list of the business cards the user has scanned.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated blue (#29ABE2) to convey trust and professionalism, suitable for a networking app.
- Background color: Light blue (#E5F5FF), a desaturated version of the primary, creates a calm and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Teal (#24A19A), an analogous color, complements the blue and adds a touch of sophistication.
- Font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, which conveys a modern and neutral look. Good for UI.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different actions and information fields.
- Employ a clean and structured layout with clear visual hierarchy for easy navigation.
- Use subtle animations to provide feedback on user interactions and improve engagement.
